# Demo flow

## Goal

Show that the product starts from a fuzzy human brief and ends with a defensible outreach deliverable.

## Suggested demo

### Step 1

Open the console and paste a brief like:

"yc로 유명한 pg인데 그가 만든 서비스 중 하나인 hacker news와 비즈니스를 하고싶음. 검색 쪽으로 wedge가 있는지 보고 싶다."

### Step 2

Write the objective and offer.

### Step 3

Click **Run due diligence**.

### Step 4

Talk through the run in this order:

- person / org / surface resolution
- why this target
- recommended wedge
- evidence labels
- subject lines
- final email

### Step 5

Copy the subject or draft.

## Demo point to emphasize

The product is not saying:

- "give me an email prompt"

It is saying:

- "tell me who you want to reach and what you want to make happen"

That is the core UX shift.
